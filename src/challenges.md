interesting to learn and provide a unique experience.

 - Rev mode
 - One rotation button
 - 10-high well
 - No RNG memory
 - Instant autoshift delay and repeat
 - Rising garbage
 - No I piece
 - Speedmods
 - Levelstop skilltest

 - Singles skilltest
